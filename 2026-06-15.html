6月15日（月）日英 洋上風力や高温ガス炉開発で協力、アメリカとイラン 双方主張曲げず「合意」か

June 15th (Mon) - Japan and the UK cooperate on offshore wind and high-temperature gas reactor development; US and Iran both refuse to back down, is an "agreement" in sight?

6月15日（月） rokugatsu jūgonicji (getsuyōbi)
June 15th (Monday)
日英 nichiei
Japan and the UK (Japan-UK)
洋上風力 yōjō fūryoku
offshore wind power
や ya
and (for listing multiple items)
高温ガス炉開発 kōon gasuro kaihatsu
high-temperature gas-cooled reactor development
で協力 de kyōryoku
cooperate in / cooperation on
アメリカとイラン amerika to iran
the United States and Iran
双方 sōhō
both sides / both parties
主張曲げず shuchō magezu
without yielding their assertions / not backing down on their claims
「合意」か "gōi" ka
is there an "agreement"? / will they reach a consensus?
